Common questions

About crumplz vs Northbeam

Can I use crumplz alongside Northbeam?
Absolutely. They solve different problems. Northbeam tells you how your budget performed historically — which is essential for attribution and future planning. crumplz catches live drifts before they become losses. Most DTC brands that use both keep Northbeam for weekly strategy and crumplz as their daily safety net.
Does crumplz replace attribution tools?
No. crumplz is not an attribution tool. It doesn't model cross-channel ROAS or build cohort reports. It monitors your active Meta campaigns in real time, detects anomalies in ROAS, CPA, CTR and MER, and sends you one actionable alert when something drifts. The job is protection, not analysis.
What does "early-warning" actually mean?
crumplz checks your campaigns every 2 hours. When a metric — say, CPA — moves outside your historical pattern, crumplz sends you an alert with the severity, the affected campaign, and the recommended action (like "pause Audience Network for 24h" or "cut budget −30% for 12h"). Average detection delay without crumplz: 4h17. With crumplz: under 2 hours.
Is it safe to connect my Meta Ads account?
Yes. crumplz uses read-only OAuth — it can see your campaign data but cannot make any changes to your campaigns, budget, or settings. Minimal permissions by design. GDPR compliant, EU hosted.
