How it works for agencies

Setup in minutes.
Protection from day one.

Step 01

Connect each client's Meta Ads

Read-only OAuth connection. Each account takes under 3 minutes. crumplz never writes to client accounts — no permission questions, no campaign risk.

Step 02

Connect each client's Shopify

Link each Shopify store for MER cross-validation. crumplz uses the store's revenue data to validate Meta performance signals and filter false alerts.

Step 03

Configure alert routing

Set per-account alert channels. Route Client A alerts to your paid team's Slack channel, Client B to your personal WhatsApp, Client C to the client's own email. Full flexibility.

Step 04

crumplz monitors, you act

Every 2 hours, crumplz checks each account. When something drifts, you get an alert with the account, the metric, the diagnosis, and the suggested action — nothing else.

FAQ

Agency monitoring questions.

Can I give my clients access to crumplz to see their own account status?
Yes. The Pro plan includes team seats which can be assigned to client-facing contacts. You control what each user can see — you can give a client visibility into their own account without exposing other client accounts. Alert routing is independent per account.
Does each client account need its own Shopify connection?
Yes — each client's Shopify store connects independently. This ensures MER cross-validation is accurate per client. The client doesn't need to do this themselves — you can connect it on their behalf using your agency's read-only Shopify Partner access.
Does crumplz require admin access to client Meta Ads accounts?
No. crumplz only requires read-only Meta Marketing API access. This is typically granted through a standard analyst or view-only role on the client's Business Manager. crumplz cannot make changes to any ad account — no risk to client campaigns.
Can we white-label crumplz alerts for our clients?
White-labelling is on the roadmap. Currently, alerts are sent from crumplz branding. If white-label agency support is important to you, reach out — we're building this based on agency demand.
