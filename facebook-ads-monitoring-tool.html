What crumplz monitors

Every metric that matters for DTC.

Facebook's native tools show you data. crumplz tells you when something is wrong — and what to do about it.

ROAS drop detection

crumplz establishes a dynamic baseline for each ad set and triggers an alert when ROAS falls outside the expected range — accounting for day-of-week and seasonal patterns.

CPA spike alert

When your cost per acquisition rises without a matching increase in revenue quality, crumplz surfaces the affected campaigns with a diagnostic reason: creative fatigue, audience exhaustion, or algorithm instability.

CTR degradation warning

A declining CTR is often the first sign of creative fatigue. crumplz catches it early — giving you time to refresh creatives before ROAS follows.

MER cross-validation

Before every alert, crumplz checks your Shopify Marketing Efficiency Ratio. If the drop in Facebook metrics is offset by other channels performing better, you won't receive a false alarm.

Budget drift detection

Ad spend that creeps above or below target is caught within hours. Whether it's a rogue campaign or an underspending ad set, crumplz flags it before the month-end reconciliation headache.

Actionable alert format

Every alert includes what drifted, by how much, for how long, and what to check first. No more digging through reports to understand whether to pause, adjust bid, or refresh creative.

FAQ

Questions about Facebook Ads monitoring.

Does crumplz work with Facebook Business Manager?
Yes. crumplz connects via the Meta Marketing API using standard OAuth. It works with any Business Manager setup including multiple ad accounts, agencies, and shared access configurations. The connection is read-only — crumplz cannot make changes to your account.
Is "Facebook Ads" the same as "Meta Ads" in crumplz?
Yes — entirely. Meta rebranded Facebook Ads as Meta Ads in 2021, but the underlying platform, API, and metrics are the same. crumplz monitors Meta Ads which covers campaigns running across Facebook, Instagram, Messenger and Audience Network.
How is crumplz different from Facebook's built-in automated rules?
Facebook's automated rules trigger actions on your campaigns — pausing, adjusting bids, changing budgets. crumplz is a monitoring tool: it detects drifts, cross-validates with your Shopify MER, and sends you an actionable alert. You make the decision and take the action. This means no automation risk, no false positives pausing healthy campaigns, and always human judgment in the loop.
Can crumplz monitor multiple Facebook ad accounts?
Yes. The Growth plan covers 3 ad accounts and the Pro plan covers unlimited ad accounts. Each account is monitored independently with its own dynamic baselines. Alerts are tagged by account so you always know which one is drifting.
