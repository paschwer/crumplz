FAQ

Slack integration questions.

Can I send alerts to multiple Slack channels?
Yes. You can configure different alert types to go to different channels — for example, ROAS drop alerts to #paid-performance and budget drift alerts to #finance-ops. You can also route alerts per ad account to separate channels if you're managing multiple brands.
Does crumplz need write access to my Slack workspace?
crumplz needs permission to post messages to the channels you configure. It does not read your Slack messages, access DMs, or touch any other channels. The OAuth scope is limited to incoming webhook posting.
Can I use both Slack and WhatsApp for the same alert?
Yes. You can enable multiple delivery channels for the same alert — useful if you want the team to see it in Slack and the founder to receive it on WhatsApp simultaneously. Each channel can be toggled independently per alert type.
