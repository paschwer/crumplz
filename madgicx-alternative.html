FAQ

crumplz vs Madgicx

Can crumplz and Madgicx work together?
Yes. They don't overlap. Madgicx handles automation and creative analytics inside your Meta Ads account. crumplz monitors performance from the outside, cross-validates with Shopify, and alerts you when something drifts — regardless of what Madgicx's rules did or didn't catch.
What if I don't want automated changes — just alerts?
Then crumplz is exactly the right tool. crumplz is read-only by design — it cannot touch your campaigns, budgets, or targeting. It detects drifts, sends you the alert and recommended action, and waits for you to decide. Human-in-the-loop by default.
Why does read-only matter?
Automation tools that write to your campaigns carry inherent risk — an overzealous rule can pause a performing campaign or misallocate budget during a false positive. crumplz's read-only architecture means it can never make a bad automated decision. The worst case is a missed alert; never a wrong action.
