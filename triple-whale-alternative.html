FAQ

Questions about switching
or complementing Triple Whale

Do I need to cancel Triple Whale to use crumplz?
No. crumplz and Triple Whale do different things. Triple Whale is an attribution and analytics platform — great at showing you the full picture after the fact. crumplz is a 24/7 early-warning system for live campaign drifts. Most DTC brands that use both call it "the brain + the alarm."
Does crumplz access my Triple Whale data?
No. crumplz connects directly to Meta Ads and Shopify via read-only OAuth. It doesn't need Triple Whale access and works independently of any analytics tool you're already using.
What's the real difference between a dashboard and crumplz?
A dashboard answers "what happened?" when you open it. crumplz answers "what's drifting right now?" without you having to ask. It's the difference between a rearview mirror and a warning light. You need both, but when the ROAS drops on a Sunday morning, only one of them wakes you up.
How quickly can I get set up?
Three minutes. Connect your Meta Ads account (read-only OAuth), connect Shopify, pick your alert channel (WhatsApp, Slack or Email), and crumplz starts watching. First check happens within 2 hours.
Does crumplz work for agencies managing multiple clients?
Yes. The Pro plan supports multiple stores, multiple ad accounts, and roles and permissions — so you can give each client their own alert channel without mixing signals. The Growth plan covers up to 3 ad accounts.
