FAQ

About crumplz alert channels

Can I use WhatsApp, Slack and Email at the same time?
Yes. You can activate all three simultaneously. Many teams do: WhatsApp for the founder or solo operator who needs to react fast, Slack for the growth team channel, and email for an audit record sent to stakeholders. Each channel gets the same alert with full detail.
How does the WhatsApp integration work?
During setup, you connect your WhatsApp number to crumplz via a verification flow. Alerts are delivered as structured messages from the crumplz WhatsApp sender. The message includes the drift metric, the severity, the affected campaign, the recommended action, and a direct link to the campaign in Meta Ads Manager.
Can I route alerts for different accounts to different people?
Yes on Pro. The Pro plan supports per-account alert routing — so alerts for Client A go to their account manager's Slack, and alerts for Client B go to a different channel and WhatsApp. On Growth, alerts from all 3 ad accounts go to the same configured channels.
How many alerts will I typically receive per week?
Most brands receive 0–3 alerts per week — crumplz only fires when a drift is confirmed by multiple signals. Healthy campaigns with stable performance will see very few alerts. You can also adjust sensitivity thresholds on Growth and Pro plans to fine-tune your signal-to-noise ratio.
